## 5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide the App and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

**Account Data:** Retained while your account is active and for 90 days after account deletion (to allow reactivation).

**Transaction Data:** Retained for 7 years for tax and accounting purposes.

**Study Content:** Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.

**Usage Logs:** Retained for 12 months for analytics and debugging.

**Cookies:** Expire per cookie settings (typically 30-90 days).

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting support@pagepay.app. Note that some data may be retained as required by law or legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).

## 10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

- Remember your preferences (theme, language)
- Track app usage and performance
- Deliver personalized ads
- Prevent fraud

**Types of Technologies:**

**Cookies:** Small text files stored on your device. We use session cookies (expire when you close the app) and persistent cookies (remain on your device).

**Local Storage:** Data stored locally in the app (preferences, cached content).

**Analytics Tools:** Usage analytics and error tracking.

**Advertising Tools:** Ad delivery and measurement.

**Managing Cookies:**

- Clear app data/cache in device settings
- Disable personalized ads:
  - Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  - iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking

Note: Disabling cookies may limit app functionality.
